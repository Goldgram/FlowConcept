Create
Reignite your imagination with activities from spray painting to knitting. No matter how old we are creativity is a form of self-expression of feelings and experiences. As we create we begin to access our thoughts, feelings and beliefs. It gives us permission to take risks, try new things, and strip away our inhibitions.
Move
Get out into nature and become fit as you swim, hike and dance. Exercise allows you to reconnect with nature and has fantastic benefits. From improving your mood and boosting your energy levels to helping with weight loss and keeping your body healthy. It allows us to get away from our day to day stresses and cleanse our brain.

Make cocktails and cookies, grow basil and chilis, enjoy it all. These simple and repetitive activities like needing bread that demand your whole attention, can have a calming, meditative effect. All these experiences also stimulate the senses, which in turn increases feel-good endorphins. You also get to share all your fantastic creations with your friends and family.

From Ashtanga yoga to mindfulness destress from your hectic life. Daily stress can take a toll on both physical and mental health. Studies show that various forms of relaxation can reduce this stress as well as restore energy and help you enjoy a better quality of life
